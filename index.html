About Us

Welcome to Narayana IIT Olympiad School, Saraswati Bhavan, Bengaluru

The Narayana IIT Olympiad School In Saraswati Bhavan, Bengaluru offers a 360-degree learning environment to its students and is rated as one of the best schools in Saraswathi Bhavan, Bengaluru. The school follows an integrated CBSE syllabus which is equipped with a micro-schedule that defines preparation on an hour-to-hour basis and acts as an added advantage to students. Further, Narayana schools follow the best and top teaching methodologies. These methods offer the best possible foundation and assistance that help students perform exceptionally well in Olympiad and other competitive exams.
